Root cause of false positives: Serper sometimes returns job listings from other companies (aggregator pages, "related jobs" sections). The AI doesn't always verify that the employer name matches the searched company.

Recommended fix: Add to agent system prompt — "Verify that the hiring company name in the job listing matches the company domain you searched for. Reject any listing where it does not match."
